About HeartGuard

Our Mission

HeartGuard is an AI-powered platform designed to help identify individuals at risk of heart disease using machine learning algorithms. By analyzing key health indicators, our system provides early warnings and risk assessments to promote proactive heart health management.

How It Works

Input Health Data

Users provide their health metrics through our secure form

AI Analysis

Multiple machine learning models analyze the input data

Risk Assessment

Detailed risk reports are generated with actionable insights
